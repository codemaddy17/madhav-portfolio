# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users
- **Primary**: Tech recruiters, engineering managers, and startup founders looking for full-stack and iOS engineering interns or developers.
- **Secondary**: Concert/event organizers, cultural fest coordinators, and community groups seeking professional event photography.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a high-end personal brand portal that bridges Madhav's primary identity as a software engineer with his creative side as a photographer, convincing visitors of his technical competence, algorithmic foundations, and meticulous eye for design.

## Positioning
A software engineer who writes robust full-stack code, executes Java DSA, and captures raw energy through a lens — proving that logical precision and creative detail come from the same voice.

## Conversion & proof
- **Primary CTA**: Direct contact form submission or email to discuss internships and collaborations.
- **Secondary CTA**: Transition to GitHub to inspect codebase quality and LinkedIn to review professional timeline.
- **The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds**: "Two crafts, one voice — engineering the invisible, capturing the moment."
- **Belief ladder**:
  1. This candidate writes solid, modern full-stack web applications (React / Node).
  2. He has strong fundamental engineering principles and algorithmic skills (Java DSA).
  3. He is meticulous, creative, and has a high attention to detail (proven by his event photography).
- **Proof on hand**:
  - Head of Media at Prayudh Debating Society (UPES), leading photography and content pipelines.
  - Software development internship at Child Help Foundation.
  - Official event photographer for Papon Concert 2025, Virasat 2024, and Doon Comedy Festival.
  - Member of Pratibimb Photography Club.

## Brand Personality
Playful, self-deprecating, and witty. A self-aware, conversational tone that leverages lighthearted humor to make the profile memorable, backed by rock-solid technical delivery.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, overstimulating, or overly-flashy animations that distract the user from reading details or navigation.
- Sterile, dry corporate layouts with stock icons and generic "value-prop" cards.
- Dark mode terminal screens that feel unapproachable or hard to read.

## Design Principles
- **Narrative Over Layout**: Use copy and personality to make the transition between "code" and "lens" feel natural and deliberate.
- **Micro-Interaction Craft**: Visual details (like the interactive custom cursor and GSAP transitions) must be highly polished to validate frontend design capabilities.
- **Honest & Grounded Delivery**: Avoid corporate fluff or empty marketing jargon; state credentials with clarity and humor.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict contrast checks (minimum 4.5:1) for body text and overlays.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` media queries with simplified CSS transitions.
- Semantic HTML tags for keyboard navigation.
